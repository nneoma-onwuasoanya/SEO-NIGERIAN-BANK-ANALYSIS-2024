# SEO VISIBILITY ANALYSIS ON NIGERIAN BANKS (2024)
Using Google keyword planner, Serp Hits and revenue data for each bank, i explored Nigeian banks performance in search engine visibility and examined the relationship between their online presence and financial success. By combining SEO metrics and financial performance data, My goal was to understand which banks dominate in search visibility and whether this correlates with their financial health.

## Business Problem
In today's digital world, strong SEO performance can play a vital role in the success of financial institutions. This analysis helps answer these business questions:

How do Nigerian banks perform in search engine results for key banking-related keywords?

Is there a correlation between SEO visibility and revenue for these banks?

How can banks optimize their SEO strategies to improve their online presence and attract more customers?

## Results 
Keywords like "Savings account," "USSD code," and "Short-term loan" were the most searched, highlighting strong demand for consumer banking.

Access Bank leads with 34 keywords and a 14.78% visibility share.

First Bank follows with 32 keywords and 13.91%.

Standard Chartered appears for 25 keywords, emphasizing SEO importance.

GTCO leads with an average rank of 3.95, showing strong performance for fewer but valuable keywords.

High visibility correlates with high revenue for Access Bank and First Bank, while GTCO shows the value of ranking well for fewer, highly competitive keywords.


The "Loan" category had the highest volume, followed by "Card/Payment" and "Digital Banking," indicating strong consumer interest in loans.

## Recommendations:
Target High-Volume Keywords like "loan" to boost visibility.

GTCO's performance suggests improving ranks for existing keywords can boost conversions.

Invest in tools for more accurate tracking and insights.

Monitor Competitors: Regularly benchmark against top performers to refine SEO strategies.
